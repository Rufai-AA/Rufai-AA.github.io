Case study from Coursera - Google Data Analytics Capstone

In this case study, I take on the role of a junior data analyst at Cyclistic, a leading bike-share company in Chicago. As a member of the marketing analysis team, our primary objective is to maximize annual memberships, a critical aspect for the company's future success. The focus of our analysis revolves around unraveling the distinctive usage patterns of casual riders and annual members of Cyclistic bikes. This case study delves into our efforts to understand these patterns and their strategic implications for the company.
